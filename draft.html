How to Play / Rules

The Pool — You can only draft superstars available in the left pool. Use the search and filters (gender, tier) to find them.

Drafting — Drag and drop superstars from the pool into the brand columns (on mobile: tap a superstar to select, then tap a brand to assign). Or use the "DRAFT" button on each brand to auto-assign.

Saving — Use the bottom bar to Export to Excel (roster data) or Finalize Draft to preview and download an HQ graphic of your draft results.
