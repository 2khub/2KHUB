About 2KHUB

2KHUB is a free, fan-built toolset for WWE 2K Universe Mode and MyGM players. No paywalls, no sign-ups — just the tools you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the WWE 2K Universe Draft Tool work?

Select any WWE 2K roster (2K26, 2K25, 2K24, AEW and more), create multiple brands with custom colors and names, then draft superstars with one click. The tool tracks all picks in real-time and lets you export the results as an image or Excel file.

Can I use this for MyGM mode?

Absolutely. Plan your entire brand roster before starting a new MyGM save. Simulate the draft, balance tiers (Main Event, Mid Card, Jobber), and ensure the right mix of superstars for your brand's identity.

Is 2KHUB free to use?

Yes — 2KHUB is completely free and always will be. It's a passion project built by wrestling fans for the community. No paywalls, no subscriptions, no hidden fees.
