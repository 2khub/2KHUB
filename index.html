The Ultimate WWE 2K Universe Mode Companion

Welcome to 2KHUB, the premier free toolset designed specifically for WWE 2K Universe players.

Custom Universe Draft: Start your brand split right. Use our interactive Draft Tool to seamlessly organize your superstars. Whether you are doing a randomized lottery or manually picking your top stars for Raw, SmackDown, AEW, or custom shows, our tool provides an up-to-date, visual roster experience. It automatically keeps track of every pick, ensuring no superstar is selected twice.

Match Card Generator: Bring your PPVs to life. Our Match Card Generator allows you to create stunning graphics for your main events and title matches in seconds. Choose your theme, select your superstars, and generate immersive graphics that make your Universe Mode feel like a real-life broadcast. It is the perfect way to share your booking on Reddit (r/WWEGames), Discord, or social media.

Built for the Community: 2KHUB is a passion project built by wrestling fans, for wrestling fans. We are dedicated to constantly updating our rosters and adding new assets so your Universe Mode always feels fresh and official.
